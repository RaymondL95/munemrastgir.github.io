Ketogenic Diet

Gets your body into ketosis which means the body’s primary energy source is fats instead of sugar. Results in high weight loss because more fats will be burned than normal. 70-75% calories from fat, 15-20% calories from protein and 5-10% calories from carbohydrates. Need to wait a few days to get used to this new nutrition plan and then wait a few weeks to fully enter the ketosis state.
